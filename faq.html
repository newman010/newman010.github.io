IS SUSHI HOUSE OPEN ON NATIONAL HOLIDAYS?
  • New Year’s Day – Open for Dinner only
  • July 4th – Gone Fishing! Closed all day
  • Labor Day – Open for Dinner only
  • Thanksgiving Day – Gone Fishing! Closed all day
  • Christmas Day – Gone Fishing! Closed all day
ANY TIPS ON BEING SEATED QUICKLY?
  • Since we open at 5:00 pm on Sundays, the wait starts at 5:00 pm.
  • Monday-Saturday, the wait normally starts from 6:00 pm and ends around 9:00 pm. So please do come in before 6:00 pm to avoid the wait.
  • The wait is significantly shorter on major game nights.
  • It is very easy to be immediately seated for lunch (weekdays only).
IS THERE A DRESS CODE?
  • No dress code, you can dress casually or dress up.
WHEN IS HAPPY HOUR?
  • Monday – Thursday – 11:30am – 2:20pm, 4:45pm – 6:00pm
  • Friday – 11:30am – 2:20pm, 4:45pm – 6:00pm
  • Saturday – 4:30pm-6:00pm
  • During happy hour you can enjoy – 2 for 1 Sake for Evening Happy Hour only.
